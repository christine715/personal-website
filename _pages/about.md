I am a Ph.D. student at Columbia University, where I am advised by <a href="https://zzzzzbbzzzzz.github.io/zhengbozou.github.io/" target="_blank">Prof. Zhengbo Zou</a> in the <a href="https://intelconstructlab.github.io/" target="_blank">Intelligent Construction (ICON) Lab</a>. My research lies at the intersection of construction automation, human–robot interaction, and human-centered artificial intelligence, with a particular interest in developing intelligent robotic systems for construction.

Before starting my Ph.D., I received my M.A.Sc. in Civil Engineering from the University of British Columbia, where my research focused on egocentric vision for intelligent construction. I earned my B.Sc. (First Class Honours) in Geomatics from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, where I worked on remote sensing, LiDAR, and geospatial data analysis.
