# About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Civil Engineering and Engineering Mechanics at Columbia University.

My research focuses on

- Human–Robot Interaction
- Construction Robotics
- Human-centered AI
- Computer Vision
- Egocentric Vision
- Worker Activity Recognition

I develop intelligent robotic systems that improve collaboration between humans and robots in construction environments through computer vision, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.
