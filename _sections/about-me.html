1. “Towards a digital-twin system for smart mobility for People with Disabilities (PwDs) using multi-data sensing and big data analytics”

Funded by Research Institute for Sustainable Urban Development (RISUD), The Hong Kong Polytechnic University.
With the advocacy of Smart City, intelligent mobility of Disabilities has aroused concerns. My work focuses on constructing 3D map and providing real time routing services for disabilities.
