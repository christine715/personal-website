1. "Towards Digital Twins: Hole Classification in LiDAR Point Clouds based on Persistent Homology and Machine Learning"

Contact me for the whole version of my BSc dissertation!
Holes in LiDAR Point Cloud, attributed to occlusions and weak reflectance, interfere with the derivatives of point clouds and their subsequent analysis while simplifying geospatial data retrieval and object detection through hole detection. The existing approaches in hole detection usually require sufficient user intervention. We proposed Persistent Homology, a computer vision based hole detection method in the point clouds with a fully automatic manner and a machine learning approach to classify the hole types detected for hole filling and geospatial data extraction with remarkable performance.


2. "Towards a digital-twin system for smart mobility for People with Disabilities (PwDs) using multi-data sensing and big data analytics"

Funded by Research Institute for Sustainable Urban Development (RISUD), The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, 2021-2024,
Dr. Xintao Liu (PI).
With the advocacy of Smart City, intelligent mobility of Disabilities has aroused concerns. My work focuses on constructing the 3D map and providing real-time navigation for disabilities.
